RED VELVET CAKE

    Recipe:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons red food coloring
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

    Preparation Steps:

  • Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  • In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  • In a separate bowl, whisk together the vegetable oil, buttermilk, eggs, food coloring, vinegar, and vanilla extract.
  • Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until well combined.
  • Pour the batter into two 9-inch round cake pans that have been greased and lined with parchment paper.
  • Bake the cakes for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  • Allow the cakes to cool completely before frosting and serving.

I hope you enjoy this red velvet cake recipe!
