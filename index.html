    Carrot cake ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup grated carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans or walnuts (optional)
    Step-by-step guide

  • Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (175 degrees Celsius). Grease and flour a 9x13 inch baking pan.
  • In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, oil, eggs, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt. Mix until well combined.
  • Stir in the grated carrots. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan.
  • Bake the cake in the preheated oven for 40-45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.
  • Let the cake cool in the pan for a few minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Important things to know !!!

    How to properly measure ingredients.

  • Use a kitchen scale for dry ingredients such as flour, sugar, and salt. This will give you the most accurate measurement and will ensure that your recipe turns out as intended.
  • Use measuring cups for liquids such as milk, water, and oil. When measuring liquids, be sure to place the measuring cup on a flat surface and read the measurement at eye level to ensure accuracy.
  • For ingredients such as butter and shortening, use a measuring cup or a kitchen scale to measure the correct amount. When using a measuring cup, be sure to pack the ingredient down firmly to ensure that you get an accurate measurement.

    How to properly preheat an oven.

  • Check the recipe you are using to determine the correct oven temperature.
  • Set your oven to the correct temperature and allow it to preheat for at least 15 minutes before placing your food in the oven.
  • Use an oven thermometer to check the temperature of your oven to ensure that it is at the correct temperature before baking.
